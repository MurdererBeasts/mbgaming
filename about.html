We were a Portuguese clan and one of the oldest in the world to play Unreal Tournament '99.
Our Clan was founded by ShadowBeast aka Assassin in 02 July 2001.
Here some highlights about our born:

--- 12 March 2001 ---
ShadowBeast offer Unreal tournament game to Intruderman work pal and started playing against each other.

--- 25 June 2001 ---
ShadowBeast invite Intruderman to create a Clan of Unreal Tournament game.
From this union borns LG (Limpeza Geral) clan.

--- 30 June 2001 ---
ShadowBeast decides change the name of the clan to something more related with his nick name.

--- 02 July 2001 ---
ShadowBeast resolves that the clan name must have the "Beast" word and ask Intruderman
to choose the second word to the clan name. It has born MB (Murderer Beasts).
